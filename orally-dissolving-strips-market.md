<p><strong>The Orally Dissolving Strips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orodispersible Film</li><li>Oromucosal Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orally dissolving strips market comprises two main types: orodispersible films and oromucosal films. Orodispersible films are designed to dissolve rapidly in saliva, delivering medication directly into the oral cavity for quick absorption. In contrast, oromucosal films are formulated for adhesion to the mucosal membranes, ensuring localized or systemic drug release. Both types offer convenience, enhanced patient compliance, and are increasingly used for various therapeutic applications, including analgesics, antiepileptics, and vitamins, catering to diverse healthcare needs.</p></p>
