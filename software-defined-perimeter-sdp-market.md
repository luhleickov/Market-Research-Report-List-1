<p><p>Software-Defined Perimeter (SDP) is a cybersecurity framework that dynamically creates a secure perimeter around applications and services, regardless of their location. It operates on the principle of "never trust, always verify," ensuring that users only gain access to specific resources they are authorized to access. By virtualizing network access and utilizing a zero-trust model, SDP enhances security by hiding infrastructure and services from unauthorized users, thus reducing the attack surface.</p><p>The SDP market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing cybersecurity threats, the rise of remote work, and the need for secure cloud services. Businesses are recognizing the importance of agile and scalable security solutions, leading to accelerated adoption across various sectors. The integration of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning into SDP solutions is also fostering innovation and enhancing security capabilities.</p><p>The Software-Defined Perimeter (SDP) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period. Overall, the SDP market is poised for robust growth as organizations seek to fortify their cybersecurity postures.</p></p>
