<p><strong>The Edible Gelatin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Preparation</li><li>Acid Preparation</li><li>Enzyme Preparation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The edible gelatin market comprises three main preparation types: alkali, acid, and enzyme. Alkali preparation involves treating collagen-rich materials with an alkaline solution, resulting in gelatin with higher viscosity and clarity. Acid preparation uses acidic solutions to extract gelatin, producing a product with specific gelling properties and lower viscosity. Enzyme preparation employs proteolytic enzymes to break down collagen, yielding gelatin with unique functional characteristics. Each type serves diverse applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics, catering to varying consumer needs.</p></p>
