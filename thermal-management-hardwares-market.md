<p><p>Thermal management hardware markets are categorized into Type I and Type II segments. Type I encompasses active thermal management solutions, such as cooling systems and heat exchangers, which require energy to operate and actively regulate temperature. Type II includes passive solutions like heat sinks and thermal insulation, relying on material properties to dissipate heat without external energy input. Both types play crucial roles in various applications, including electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of systems.</p></p>
