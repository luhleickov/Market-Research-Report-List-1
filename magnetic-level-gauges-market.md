<p><strong>The Magnetic Level Gauges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic level gauges are specialized devices used to measure the fluid levels in various industrial applications. The market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact magnetic level gauges involve a float that rises and falls with the liquid level, directly contacting the fluid for accurate readings. In contrast, non-contact magnetic level gauges utilize technologies like radar or ultrasonic waves to measure level without physical contact, offering advantages in high-temperature or corrosive environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
