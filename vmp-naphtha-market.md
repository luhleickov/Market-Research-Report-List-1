<p><p>VM&P Naphtha, or Variable Mix & Purity Naphtha, is categorized into two primary market types: Heavy Naphtha and Light Naphtha. Heavy Naphtha is typically used in the production of petrochemicals and for blending in gasoline, containing higher boiling point hydrocarbons that enhance energy content. In contrast, Light Naphtha consists of lighter fractions, often utilized as a solvent or for gasoline blending, characterized by lower boiling point hydrocarbons, leading to a quicker evaporation rate and cleaner-burning properties. Both serve distinct industrial applications.</p></p>
