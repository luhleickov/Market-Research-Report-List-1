<p><ul><li>Primary Antibody</li><li>Secondary Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyclonal antibodies market comprises two main types: primary and secondary antibodies. Primary antibodies are designed to bind specifically to a target antigen, serving as the first line of detection in various assays like ELISA and Western blotting. Secondary antibodies, on the other hand, are used to bind to the primary antibodies, often conjugated with labels for visualization or amplification, enabling enhanced signal detection. Together, they play a crucial role in research, diagnostics, and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
