<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) pumping systems for irrigation utilize solar energy to operate pumps, which are crucial for water access in agricultural settings. The market is primarily categorized into submersible and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are installed underwater, making them ideal for deep wells, efficiently delivering water from significant depths. Conversely, surface pumps are positioned above ground, suitable for extracting water from shallow sources like rivers or ponds. Both types enhance irrigation efficiency and provide sustainable solutions for water management in farming.</p></p>
