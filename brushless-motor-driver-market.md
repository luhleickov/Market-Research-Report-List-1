<p><ul><li>Surface Magnetic Pole</li><li>Embedded Magnetic Pole</li><li>Annular Magnetic Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brushless motor driver market can be categorized into three main types based on magnetic pole configurations: Surface Magnetic Pole, Embedded Magnetic Pole, and Annular Magnetic Pole. Surface Magnetic Pole motors utilize magnets mounted on the motor's surface, offering a simple design and high efficiency. Embedded Magnetic Pole motors feature magnets embedded within the rotor, enhancing torque and compactness. Annular Magnetic Pole motors have a ring-shaped configuration, providing uniform magnetic fields and superior performance in various applications, such as robotics and automotive systems.</p></p>
