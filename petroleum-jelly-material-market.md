<p><p>The petroleum jelly market is categorized into two primary types: white petroleum jelly and yellow petroleum jelly. White petroleum jelly is refined and has a clear, odorless appearance, making it ideal for cosmetic and personal care applications, such as moisturizers and lip balms. Yellow petroleum jelly, on the other hand, retains some of its natural color and impurities, suited for industrial uses like machinery lubrication and as an ointment base in pharmaceuticals. Both types serve distinct markets based on their formulations and applications.</p></p>
