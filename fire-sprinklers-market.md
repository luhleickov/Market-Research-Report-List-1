<p><strong>The Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Bubbles</li><li>Fusible Alloy</li><li>Stent Type</li><li>Heat Sink Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire sprinkler market comprises various types, including glass bulb, fusible alloy, stent type, and heat sink type sprinklers. Glass bulb sprinklers activate when the glass shatters due to heat, releasing water. Fusible alloy sprinklers use a low-melting metal to trigger water flow. Stent type sprinklers feature a mechanical design that opens at specific temperatures, while heat sink type sprinklers utilize a heat-absorbing material to delay activation, ensuring a targeted response to intense fires. Each type offers distinct advantages for fire safety solutions.</p></p>
