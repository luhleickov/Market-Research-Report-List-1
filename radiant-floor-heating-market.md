<p><p>The radiant floor heating market is primarily divided into two types: electric and hydronic systems. Electric radiant heating utilizes electrical cables or mats installed beneath the flooring to generate warmth, making it suitable for smaller areas or retrofitting. In contrast, hydronic systems circulate heated water through pipes installed under the floor, providing a more energy-efficient solution for larger spaces. Both types offer comfort and energy savings, catering to various residential and commercial heating needs.</p></p>
