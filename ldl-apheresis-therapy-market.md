The market is primarily segmented into two types: Double Filtration LDL Apheresis, which uses a two-step filtration process to selectively remove LDL particles while retaining other blood components; and Cascade Filtration LDL Apheresis, which employs a cascade mechanism to separate LDL from plasma proteins. Both approaches aim to improve patient outcomes and manage hyperlipidemia effectively.</p></p>
