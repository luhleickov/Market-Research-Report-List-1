<p><p>The aviation refueling market encompasses several key segments: Mobile Tankers, Hydrant Dispensers, and Fueling Cabinets/Skids. Mobile Tankers are versatile vehicles that transport and dispense fuel directly to aircraft on the tarmac. Hydrant Dispensers are fixed systems providing fuel access through underground pipelines, offering efficient refueling at airports. Fueling Cabinets/Skids are compact, portable units used for smaller operations, ensuring safe storage and dispensing of fuel. Together, these solutions enhance operational efficiency and safety in aircraft refueling processes.</p></p>
