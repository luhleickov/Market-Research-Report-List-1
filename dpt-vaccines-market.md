<p><p>The DPT vaccine market includes three main types: DTaP, Tdap, and DTwP. DTaP is a pediatric vaccine for children under seven, protecting against diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis. Tdap is a booster for adolescents and adults, also covering the same diseases but formulated for older populations. DTwP, a whole-cell vaccine, offers protection against diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis but is less commonly used due to side effects. Each type serves distinct age groups and serves vital public health roles.</p></p>
