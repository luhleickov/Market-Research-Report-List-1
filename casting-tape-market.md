<p><p>The casting tape market comprises three main types: Gypsona (Plaster of Paris), Fiberglass, and Polyester. Gypsona is ideal for lightweight applications and offers excellent moldability, commonly used in orthopedic casts. Fiberglass casts are known for their strength, moisture resistance, and lightweight properties, making them popular in medical settings. Polyester casts provide durability and flexibility, often used in both medical and industrial applications. Each type serves specific needs, balancing factors such as weight, strength, and moisture resistance for effective casting solutions.</p></p>
