<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) is an automotive technology that optimizes engine performance, efficiency, and emissions by adjusting the timing of the valve openings and closings. The VVT market can be categorized into two types: Discrete (Stepped) Adjustment, which offers fixed settings for valve timing at predetermined intervals, and Continuous (Infinite) Adjustment, allowing for seamless and variable control of valve timing across a range of engine speeds and loads, enhancing performance and fuel efficiency for diverse driving conditions.</p></p>
