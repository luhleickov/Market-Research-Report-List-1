<p><p>The wireless smart lighting controls system market features several key technologies, each with unique characteristics. Zigbee is known for low power consumption and mesh networking, ideal for extensive installations. Wi-Fi offers high data transfer rates, integrating easily with existing networks but consuming more energy. Bluetooth provides simple, short-range connectivity, suitable for personal or small-scale applications. Z-Wave is designed for home automation, focusing on reliable communication in low-power devices. EnOcean utilizes energy harvesting, enabling self-powered sensors for sustainable smart lighting solutions.</p></p>
