<p><ul><li>Dry Seasoning</li><li>Wet Seasoning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BBQ seasoning market comprises two primary types: dry seasoning and wet seasoning. Dry seasoning typically includes a blend of spices, herbs, and salts, often packaged in shaker containers for easy application. It offers a concentrated flavor that enhances grilled meats and vegetables. In contrast, wet seasoning refers to marinades and sauces combining spices with oils, vinegar, or other liquids, providing moisture and flavor penetration during cooking. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences in outdoor cooking and grilling experiences.</p></p>
