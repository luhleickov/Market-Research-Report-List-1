<p><p>The digital protective relay market encompasses various types, each serving specific protective functions. Overcurrent protection relays detect excessive current levels, safeguarding equipment from damage. Arc protection relays identify electrical arcing, enhancing safety in installations. Motor protection relays monitor motor health, preventing overheating and failure. Transformer protection relays ensure transformer integrity by detecting faults. Capacitor bank protection relays manage and protect capacitor banks from overvoltage or current issues. Other market segments may include specialized relays for generators, transmission lines, and industrial applications, reflecting diverse protective needs.</p></p>
