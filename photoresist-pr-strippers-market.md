<p><strong>The Photoresist (PR) Strippers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li><li>Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist (PR) strippers market is segmented into two primary types: positive photoresist strippers and negative photoresist strippers. Positive photoresist strippers are designed to effectively remove the exposed areas of photoresist, enabling precise patterning in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, negative photoresist strippers target the unexposed regions, removing the unexposed photoresist while retaining the patterned areas. Both types are essential for the photolithography process, facilitating the creation of intricate designs on wafers in electronics and microfabrication industries.</p></p>
