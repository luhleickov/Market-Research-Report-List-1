<p><p>The ankle monitors market includes two main types: Radio Frequency (RF) and Global Positioning System (GPS). RF ankle monitors operate using radio signals to track individuals within a defined area, typically useful for monitoring compliance with curfews or restricted zones. In contrast, GPS ankle monitors provide real-time location tracking through satellite signals, allowing for broader geographic monitoring. Both types are crucial for law enforcement and rehabilitation programs, enhancing public safety by ensuring accountability and tracking offender movements.</p></p>
