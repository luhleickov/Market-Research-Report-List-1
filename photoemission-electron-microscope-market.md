<p><p>The Photoemission Electron Microscope (PEEM) market includes two primary types: Ultraviolet Photoemission Electron Microscopes (U-PEEM) and X-ray Photoemission Electron Microscopes (X-PEEM). U-PEEM utilizes ultraviolet light to excite electrons from a sample's surface, allowing for high-resolution imaging of electronic structures and surface properties. In contrast, X-PEEM employs X-rays, offering deeper insights into the elemental and chemical composition of materials, along with increased sensitivity for bulk analysis. Both types advance material science and nanotechnology research through detailed surface characterization.</p></p>
