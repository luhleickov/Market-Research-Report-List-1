<p><p>The Current Feedback Operational Amplifier (CFOA) market is segmented into two main types: positive feedback and negative feedback. Positive feedback amplifies input signals, leading to increased gain and potential instability, often used in oscillators and signal conditioning. In contrast, negative feedback stabilizes gain, reduces distortion, and improves linearity, making it ideal for linear applications such as filters and amplifiers. Both types cater to various electronic applications, driving innovation and efficiency in circuit designs across numerous industries.</p></p>
