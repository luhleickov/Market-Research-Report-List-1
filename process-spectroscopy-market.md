<p><p>Process spectroscopy encompasses various market segments, including molecular, mass, and atomic spectroscopy. Molecular spectroscopy focuses on the interaction of light with molecular bonds, aiding in the analysis of organic compounds. Mass spectroscopy measures the mass-to-charge ratio of ions, providing insights into molecular structure and composition. Atomic spectroscopy involves analyzing the electromagnetic radiation absorbed or emitted by atoms, allowing for the identification and quantification of elements. Together, these techniques enhance material characterization across industries like pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p></p>
