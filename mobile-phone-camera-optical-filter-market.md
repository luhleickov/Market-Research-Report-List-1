<p><p>The mobile phone camera optical filter market includes various types designed to enhance image quality. Bandpass filters allow certain wavelengths to pass while blocking others, optimizing color capture. Longpass filters transmit longer wavelengths, often improving low-light performance and reducing glare. Shortpass filters do the opposite, filtering out longer wavelengths to enhance detail and contrast in specific light conditions. Other filters may include specialized types like neutral density or polarizing filters, catering to diverse photographic needs and applications.</p></p>
