<p><p>The pH strips market consists of various indicator types, including Methyl Red, Bromocresol Green, and Thymol Blue, each with distinct properties for measuring acidity and alkalinity. Methyl Red is effective in acidic ranges, transitioning from red to yellow between pH 4.4 and 6.2. Bromocresol Green covers a broader range, shifting from yellow to blue between pH 3.8 and 5.4. Thymol Blue operates in two ranges, indicating pH changes from red to yellow (1.2-2.8) and yellow to blue (8.0-9.6), catering to diverse applications in laboratories and industries.</p></p>
