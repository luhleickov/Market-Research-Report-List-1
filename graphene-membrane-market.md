<p><p>The graphene membrane market is categorized into two main types: single-layer and multi-layer membranes. Single-layer graphene membranes consist of a single atomic layer, offering exceptional properties such as high permeability and selectivity, making them ideal for applications in filtration and desalination. Multi-layer graphene membranes, composed of multiple stacked layers, provide enhanced mechanical strength and stability, suitable for applications requiring robust performance, such as sensors and energy storage. Both types leverage graphene's unique properties for various industrial uses.</p></p>
