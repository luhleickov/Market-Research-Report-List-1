<p><strong>The Coated Sand Core Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Method</li><li>Thermal Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coated sand core market is primarily segmented into two types: the cold method and the thermal method. The cold method involves the use of cold-setting resins to bind sand particles, providing improved strength and surface finish at lower temperatures. In contrast, the thermal method employs heat to cure the resin, resulting in faster production cycles and higher dimensional accuracy. Both methods are essential for various applications in foundries, impacting the quality and efficiency of metal casting processes.</p></p>
